Hogwarts house

In the early days of Hogwarts, the four founders selected students for their homes. Then they worry about how students will be chosen after their death, when Godric Gryffindor took off his hat and then "poured a little bit of his mind" into it to form the Sorting Hat. Sorting on behalf of them selects students for each future home. From there, at the beginning of each school year, the magical Sorting Hat will be placed on top of every new student during the Sorting Ceremony.
